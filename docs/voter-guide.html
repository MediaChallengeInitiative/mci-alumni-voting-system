1 How to Register

Create Your Account

  • Visit vote.mcialumni.com
  • Click "Don't have an account? Register here"
  • Enter your Username (choose your own, e.g., ebahindi)
  • Enter your First Name (e.g., Emmanuel)
  • Enter your Last Name (e.g., Bahindi)
  • Create a Password (minimum 8 characters)
  • Confirm your password
  • Click "Create Account"
2 How to Login

Access Your Account

  • Visit vote.mcialumni.com
  • Enter your Username
  • Enter your Password
  • Click "Cast Your Vote"
3 How to Vote

Cast Your Votes

  • Browse through the award categories
  • Select your preferred nominee for each category
  • Review your selections
  • Click "Submit Vote" to confirm
  • You will see a confirmation message when successful
