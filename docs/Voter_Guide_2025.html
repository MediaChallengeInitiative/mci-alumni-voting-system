Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I forgot my Voter ID. What do I do?

A: Contact the registration desk. They can look up your Voter ID using your name.

Q: The password doesn't work. Help!

A: Make sure you type exactly: AwardsNight2025 (it's case-sensitive). If it still doesn't work, ask an admin to reset your password.

Q: Can I vote from my phone?

A: Yes! The system works on smartphones, tablets, and computers.

Q: I started voting but got interrupted. Can I continue?

A: Yes, if you haven't submitted yet. Just log in again from the same device and continue.

Q: Can I change my vote after submitting?

A: No. Once submitted, your vote is final. Please use the Preview feature to review before submitting.

Q: How do I know my vote was counted?

A: You'll see a success message with confetti animation. You can also log in again to view your recorded ballot.
